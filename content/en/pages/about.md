Benedikt maintains a career in the world of software solutions
and education, complemented by his own competitive sports background.

His philosophy of promoting professional value creation through continuous
learning, as well as his efforts to redefine the
working world, are central elements of his career.

The core skillset consists of a combination of web development stack around Next.js 15, creative and methodical thinking frameworks on how to 
solve problems and build products and services.  

He is currently self-employed and part of Finrate AG in St.
Gallen, Switzerland. Originally from Offenbach (GER), he is a family man,
educated in Germany, the UK, and USA, multilingual, and currently lives in Freiburg im Breisgau (GER).

Benedikt spoke, moderated, and represented at international and national conferences, workshops, and gatherings. He got nominated for 
book contracts and has been published as an author and co-author. He also got interviewed by leading press outlets about his work.
